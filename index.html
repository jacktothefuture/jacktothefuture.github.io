Climate Vault

Did you know that companies have to buy permits in order to produce pollution? Climate Vault is an organization dedicated to buying up those permits and locking them away so that companies are forced to produce their products without harming the environment. It takes an organization to buy those permits, so donations towards this one can help remove literal tonnes of C02 from the environment.
